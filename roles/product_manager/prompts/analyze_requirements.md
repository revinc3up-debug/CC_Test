## Your Task
1. Extract and categorize all **functional requirements** — prioritize them (Must/Should/Could/Won't).
2. Identify **non-functional requirements** (performance, security, scalability, accessibility).
3. List all **assumptions** you are making and flag them for validation.
4. Identify **open questions** that need answers before development can start.
5. Assess **risks** — what could go wrong, and what are the mitigation strategies?
6. Suggest an **MVP scope** — the smallest set of features that delivers core value.
