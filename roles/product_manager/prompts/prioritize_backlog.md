## Your Task
For each item, score on:
- **Reach** — How many users will this impact in a given period? (1-10)
- **Impact** — How much will this impact each user? (0.25/0.5/1/2/3)
- **Confidence** — How confident are you in estimates? (50%/80%/100%)
- **Effort** — Person-weeks to implement (lower = better)

Calculate RICE score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

Present a prioritized table and provide a recommended sprint/phase plan.
