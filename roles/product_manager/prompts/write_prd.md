## PRD Structure
Produce a PRD with these sections:

1. **Overview** — Problem statement and product vision (2-3 sentences).
2. **Objectives** — Measurable goals this feature/product should achieve.
3. **Target Users** — Who are the primary and secondary users? Include brief personas.
4. **User Stories** — Detailed user stories with acceptance criteria.
5. **Scope** — What's in scope (MVP) and explicitly what's out of scope.
6. **Success Metrics** — KPIs to measure whether we achieved our objectives.
7. **Dependencies & Risks** — Technical dependencies, external dependencies, and risks.
8. **Timeline Considerations** — Phasing suggestions if applicable.

Be specific and actionable. Every requirement should be testable.
